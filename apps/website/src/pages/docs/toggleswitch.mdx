Toggle switches are instant action components used to toggle an option instantly with a single click
or tap. They are more common in mobile layouts, but are also present in desktop environments. For example,
on mobile, you can go to the settings menu and enable or disable wi-fi, airplane mode and sound with
a single tap using the associated toggle switch.
